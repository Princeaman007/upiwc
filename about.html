ABOUT

We are a branch of The Church of Pentecost. A young and vibrant church in the city of Brussels.

VISION STATEMENT

To become a global Pentecostal church that is culturally relevant in vibrant evangelism, church planting,discipleship and holistic ministry.

MISSION STATEMENT

We exist to establish responsible and self-sustaining churches filled with committed,Spirit-filled christians of character, who will impact their communities.

CORE VALUES

CONSISTENT BIBLE TEACHING (Acts 2:42a).

The Church upholds the teaching of sound biblical doctrine and does not tolerate the propagation of wrong/false doctrine. All types of prayer such as thanksgiving; worship (adoration); supplication; intercession (Eph. 6:18-19; 1 Tim. 2:1-2).

EVANGELISM

It is the presentation of Jesus Christ in the power of the Holy Spirit with the view that people will trust Jesus as Saviour and Lord and serve Him in the fellowship of the Church. Evangelism is the responsibility of every Church member and Minister.

PRAYER

We believe in the power of prayer. We see prayer as a communication between man and God. Prayer is a good opportunity to speak to God and to hear from God.
