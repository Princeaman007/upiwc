CHILDREN MINISTRY

The Children’s Ministry trains children along biblical precepts of balanced growth. This includes lessons on Salvation, Worship and Obedience. It supports such training with games, quizzes and Sword Drills.

YOUTH & PENSA MINISTRY

The Youth and PENSA Ministry takes care of the youth of the Church. It meets on Mondays for Bible studies and discussions. Aside bible studies and prayer meetings, the youth ministry engages in indoor and outdoor activities such as picnics, sports, etc.

WOMEN’S MINISTRY

The women’s ministry embraces all the womenfolk. The ministry organises retreats, seminars, workshops, lectures, and symposia in all spheres of life, including marriage enrichment, wives’ responsibilities at home, child welfare, care, and education.

MEN’S MINISTRY

The men’s ministry caters for the interest of all the men in the Church. The ministry organises seminars, workshops, lectures and symposia on all aspects of life including: marriage enrichment, husbands’ responsibilities at home, child care and education; business management and techniques; leadership and responsibility, etc.

EVANGELISM MINISTRY

This Ministry undertakes street evangelism, gospel rallies, crusades, conventions, house-to-house evangelism, and other forms of evangelism, etc. These include preaching, witnessing, winning and discipling souls.

PRAYER

Some of the normal practices are regular prayer for the Holy Spirit baptism with the initial evidence of speaking in tongues. Emphasis is placed on the fruit and gifts of the Spirit in the life of the believer. Prayers are said for healing and deliverance of the afflicted as part of the initial exercises for salvation. Services are marked as truly Pentecostal with praise and worship, teaching, exercise of gifts, prayer, testimonies, etc.
