SUNDAY SERVICE

Our Sunday services are filled with worship, praise and the word of God. We meet at 10:45am each Sunday to worship and praise our God together. We welcome everyone from all cultures and nationalities.

BIBLE STUDIES & SEMINARS

The church engages effectively in weekly Bible Studies and monthly Bible Seminars. During these teachings, we discuss various topics in the word of God.

PRAYER MEETINGS

There are regular prayer meetings held each week. We believe in the power of prayer and the act of waiting upon the Lord.

SOCIAL ACTIVITIES

We frequently organize social and outdoor activities such as picnics, sports, camp meetings, dinner etc.
